Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

one 9-inch pie 30 min-prep time 40 min-total 8 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp all purpose flower
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tbsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons, jucied and zeted
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 (9 inch) pie curst, baked
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tbsp white sugar

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Make Lemon Filling: In a medium saucepan
    • hwisk together 1 cup sugar, flour, cournstarch, and salt.
    • stir in water, lemon juice and lemon zest.
    • place egg yolk mixture back into remaining sugar mixture.
    • bring to a boil and continue to cook while stirring constantly until thinck.
    • remove form heat.
    • pour filling into baked pastry shell.
  3. Make Meringue:in a large glass or metal bowl...
    • whip egg whites until faomy
    • add sugar gradully, and contine to whip until stiff peaks from.
    • spread meringue over pie, sealing the edges at the crust.
  4. Bake:Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until meringue is golden brown
