Scrambled eggs

this is a recipie for scrambled eggs

ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream
Method
  1. melt butter in a fring pan over meduim heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 secconds (as if your aer making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate untill served)
